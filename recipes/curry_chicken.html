Curry Chicken

Description

This is a local favorite in my country. Curry chicken and white
rice. My island kinfolk tend to love curry in general, but curry chicken
is definitely a favorite.

Ingredients

  • chopped chicken
  • maggi chicken season it up
  • curry powder
  • onion
  • scotch bonnet pepper
  • green onions
  • thyme
  • irish potato
  • cooking oil
  • garlic
  • jasmine or basmati rice
  • water
  • salt
  • butter

Steps

  1. heat pan then add 2 table-spoons of cooking oil
  2. when oil is hot add generous amount of curry powder
  3. let curry heat in oil until dark but not burnt
  4. season chicken with generous portion of maggi seasoning
  5. add seasoned chicken to heated oil and curry mix
  6. stir the mix around
  7. place lid on pan to allow steaming
  8. add 2 cups of water in another pot
  9. add 1 tea spoon of salt to water
  10. cover pot and allow water to boil
  11. chop up onions, green onions, garlic and pepper
  12. dice the irish potato
  13. add all chopped and diced ingredients to the curry chicken
  14. add the thyme the curry chicken as well
  15. add a cup of rice to the boiling water
  16. add two table-spoons of butter to rice mix
